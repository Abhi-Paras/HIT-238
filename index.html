Mental Health Australia

Mental Health Australia is a non-profit organisation that educates the public about mental illness and strives to change public perceptions of mentally ill individuals in order to eliminate negative stereotypes.

Mental Health Foundation of Australia (Victoria)

The Mental Health Foundation of Australia is Australia's oldest mental health organisation, having been founded in Victoria over eight decades ago. This organisation helps professionals, patients, and concerned family members who are directly touched by mental illness. The group's goal is to provide information and collaborate with the government to guarantee that mental health caregivers' practise and training meet high standards.
