  • headspace Darwin

    phone: (08) 8931 5999
    email: headspacedarwin@anglicare-nt.org.au

    headspace Darwin is a free and confidential programme for young people aged 12 to 25 who need help with their mental health and wellbeing. Mental health, physical and sexual health, alcohol and other drugs (AOD), and vocational support are all areas where headspace Darwin may help (Work and Study). headspace Darwin has three services: headspace Primary, headspace Early Psychosis, and Enhanced Care.

  • Team Health

    phone: 1800 832 600

    TeamTALK is accessible from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m., seven days a week. Person-centered and strengths-based psychosocial assistance is provided over the phone. We recognise that you are the expert in your own life, and we also recognise that everyone of us has abilities that we may not be aware of but which may help us. Individual requirements determine whether support is provided on a one-time basis or via a series of sessions.
